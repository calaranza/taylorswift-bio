About Taylor Swift

Taylor Alison Swift is an award-winning and sensational singer-songwriter born on December 13, 1989 at West Reading, Pennsylvania, United States of America. She started her musical career in 2005 when she signed a contract with Big Machine Records, leading to the release of her self-titled debut album in October 2006. She is a multi-talented artist who has touched millions of lives around the globe through her narrative and poetic style of songwriting. Taylor Swift made a huge impact in the music industry, influenced modern pop culture and new artists, and uplifted local economies with The Eras Tour.
